Seventeen years of FREE dancing in Seattle parks!

Join us for Dancing til Dusk 2024, 17 summer evenings of free music, dance lessons and dancing in downtown Seattle parks!

1st hour is a beginning dance lesson, no partner necessary as we change partners in the lesson. If you prefer not to change partners, bring one along!
